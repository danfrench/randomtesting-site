Questions before you start?

A few quick answers for teams evaluating Randomizer Online.

Can I import employees from Excel?

Yes. Randomizer Online supports Excel and CSV imports using a standard employee list format. Easily import employee lists exported from HR systems such as Workday or PeopleSoft.

Can I exclude previous selections?

Yes. You can exclude prior selected employees from recent matching runs to help manage repeat selections.

Does it create audit reports?

Yes. Saved runs can produce printable Compliance Audit Reports with record IDs, timestamps, and performed-by tracking.

Can multiple users share an organization?

Yes. Enterprise accounts support company users, shared organization data, and administrative controls.
